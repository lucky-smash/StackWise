# Go-To-Market Strategy

## Target User

The ideal target user is:
- startup founders
- engineering managers
- technical leads
- AI-heavy product teams

especially at:
- seed to Series A stage startups
- remote engineering teams
- fast-moving AI startups

These companies often adopt multiple AI tools quickly without optimizing spend.
