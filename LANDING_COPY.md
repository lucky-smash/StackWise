# FAQ

## What does StackWise do?
StackWise audits your AI tool subscriptions and identifies potential savings opportunities.

## Does StackWise require login?
No. Users can run audits instantly without creating an account.

## How accurate are the savings estimates?
The estimates are based on current public pricing and common startup usage patterns.

## Does StackWise replace financial software?
No. It is designed specifically for AI tooling optimization and subscription analysis.

## Why does StackWise ask for company details?
For users interested in personalized optimization help and follow-up recommendations.
