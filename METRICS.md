# Metrics

## North Star Metric

Completed audits per week.

This metric directly measures whether startups are finding enough value in the product to finish the audit process.

---

# Input Metrics

## 1. Audit Completion Rate
Tracks how many visitors complete the full audit flow.

## 2. Lead Conversion Rate
Percentage of users submitting lead capture forms after viewing results.

## 3. Share Rate
Tracks how often users share or revisit audit results.

---

# Initial Instrumentation

The first analytics events I would track:
- Audit started
- Audit completed
- Lead form submitted
- CTA clicks
- Share link copied

---

# Pivot Indicator

If audit completion rates remain low after multiple UI iterations, it may indicate:
- excessive friction
- weak perceived value
- confusing onboarding

That would trigger a redesign of the onboarding and audit experience.
